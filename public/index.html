Welcome to VoteXX

VoteXX is a joint effort by xx network and an international team of academics, aimed at creating a massively scalable, end-to-end voter verifiable, and coercion-resistant electronic voting system.

Our Features

Privacy

Each voter's ballot privacy is encrypted, ensuring that no individual can know the voting selection of any voter.

Remote Voting

Our voting system enables secure, Internet-based remote voting that can be effortlessly accessed.

Coercion-resistance

Our scheme protects the voter against a strong coercer who learns all of the voter's keys via the nullification action.
